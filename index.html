My Commitment

Starting May 2, these are the commitments you can hold me accountable for.

Financial Accountability

I will ensure every tax dollar is spent responsibly and transparently, with a focus on keeping costs fair for residents.

  • Careful review of district budgets and expenses
  • Avoid unnecessary tax increases whenever possible
  • Oversight of contracts and vendor spending
  • Clear communication on where your money goes

Infrastructure & Water Reliability

Reliable water and drainage systems are essential. I will focus on maintaining and improving the infrastructure our community depends on.

  • Proactive maintenance to prevent service disruptions
  • Long-term planning for system upgrades
  • Focus on water quality and reliability
  • Preparation for growth and future demand

Transparent, Community-Focused Leadership

Residents deserve to be informed and heard. I am committed to open communication and active engagement with the community.

  • Regular updates on projects and decisions
  • Accessible and responsive to residents
  • Encouraging community input and feedback
  • Decisions made with residents’ best interests first

About Me

Hello, I am Derek Coleman

Fort Bend M.U.D. District 23 Candidate

I am a candidate for the MUD 23 Board of Directors, committed to responsible budgeting, reliable infrastructure, and transparent communication with residents. I bring over 20 years of professional experience in banking and technology, with a strong focus on financial oversight and operational efficiency. I spent more than a decade in banking, advancing to Branch Manager and gaining hands-on experience managing budgets, lending, and customer service operations—skills directly relevant to overseeing district finances. For the past decade, I have worked in information technology leadership, currently serving as a Lead Application Administrator at a Architecture Firm. In this role, I manage critical systems, support large-scale operations, and lead projects that improve efficiency and reliability—experience that translates to maintaining and strengthening essential community infrastructure. I hold an MBA in Leadership from Lamar University and maintain certifications in security, cloud administration, and endpoint management. As a Fort Bend County resident, I am focused on ensuring MUD 23 operates efficiently, keeps residents informed, and delivers dependable services today and into the future.

Transparency & Communication
